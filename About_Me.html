Here is a little about me. I am a young 2nd generation woman who grew up moving around to many different amazing places. My love for crafting comes from my family's rich history of creativity and artistry.

As a child, I was fascinated by my grandparents' talent for making beautiful things out of seemingly nothing. My parents inherited their creative spirit, and as a result, I was exposed to a world of crafting and creating from a very young age. I learned how to make things with my hands, and soon discovered that creating was not only fun, but also deeply satisfying.

Today, using my hands to craft things on a daily basis is my passion. Whether it's making beautiful handmade bracelets or cooking up delicious recipes, I put my heart and soul into every project I undertake. I believe that there's something special about creating things from scratch, and I take pride in knowing that each piece I make is truly one-of-a-kind.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and my passion for creating handmade bracelets. I hope that my love for crafting shines through in every piece, and that you find something special to add to your own jewelry collection.
